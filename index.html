Frequently Asked Questions

Is a background check required?

While not mandatory, we strongly recommend that all users complete a third-party background check through our trusted partner, Authenticate.com. Profiles indicate when a check has been completed with a "Verified" badge. You can also request that an applicant (or homeowner) gets a background check.

Who are the renters on RoomThere?

To ensure maturity and compatibility, our platform is exclusively for graduate students and young professionals. We do not accept undergraduate students.

Are renters expected to provide healthcare?

Absolutely not. RoomThere is designed for community and mutual support, not medical care. Renters are housemates, and any help is entirely optional and agreed upon in advance, never medical in nature.

How are rent and agreements handled?

RoomThere is a connection service. Once you've found a match, you will work directly with them to sign a rental agreement and arrange payments. We recommend consulting a legal professional for your lease.

How much does it cost?

There are no fees to list or search for a room. Participating on RoomThere is completely free.
